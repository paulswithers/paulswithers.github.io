My choice of Vaadin is because my preferred language of development is Java. Vaadin has been pretty easy to pick up after XPages, the documentation has a high quality, the community has been impressively open and vibrant, the Vaadin developers and developer advocates have been very welcoming and engaged with the community, and the company (not surprisingly) are committed to the product.
