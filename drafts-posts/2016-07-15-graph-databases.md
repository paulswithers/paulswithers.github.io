title: "Graph Databases, Tinkerpop and Domino"
date: 2016-07-15
---
In terms of graph, I like the conciseness of the framed graph data schema from Tinkerpop 2 (dropped for Tinkerpop 3, but there are open source framing implementations), and I like the native Java graph API in OpenNTF Domino API. I've been looking at alternative graph APIs that are also based on Tinkerpop. I wasn't impressed by IBM Graph, struggled with OrientDb, so I'm looking now at Apache Cassandra.
